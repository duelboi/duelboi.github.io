Popping Styles




Fun Fact: Popping has different styles which gives off different vibes

Animation:

Animation is meant to simulate animated characters moving frame by frame

- This technique involves abrupt tensing of the muscles to create a stop-motion illusion


Boogaloo:

Boogaloo which was invented by Boogaloo Sam

- Is a loose, fluid motion that gives the impresssion of the body having no bones

- This style incorporates isolated rolls of the body parts like head, chest, hips and legs


Miming:

Miming is a style that is inspired by classic mimes on the street

- Miming utilizes isoltations to create an illusion of an object that isn't actually there


Robot/Botting:

- Robot/Botting is when the dancer imitates a robot's mechanical movement


Strutting:

- Strutting is a style that involves hitting angles with your limbs as you hit
