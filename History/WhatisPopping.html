What is Popping?




Definition:

- Popping includes the Quick contract and relax muscles to create a jerking effect (a pop or hit) in the body


History:

- The popping dance style was invented by a dancer named Boogaloo Sam as he was watching dancers who were locking on television.

- He then created popping which is a combination of rigid-robotic movement and loose flowing moves in Fresno, California
