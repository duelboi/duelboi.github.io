Famous Poppers




There are many poppers who famous, but all could not be included*


Mr Wiggles:

Mr Wiggles is one of the original poppers who started dancing from the early 70s

- Part of the group (The Electric Boogaloos) that was responsible for the spread popping and electric boogaloo

- Judged for many competitions

Bruno "Pop n Taco" Falcon (1964 ~ 2022)

Bruno Falcon was an actor, choreographer and dancer in the 80s

- Performed alongside several musicians and choreographers over the course of his career.

- known for his collaborations with Michael Jackson, particularly his role in the Smooth Criminal music video

- He taught to the popstar the "anti-gravity lean".

Nelson (France):

Nelson is a popular popper

- Judged for many competitions like KingofDance(KOD) Australia

- The winner for Juste Debout 2018

Christian Triventi (Popping'C) (Austrian):

Popping'C is a respected popper

- Judged for many competitions like Dance Vision Vol 8

- Finalist that paired with Ness and went up against Nelson and Greenteck in Juste Debout 2018

Boogaloo Kite (Japan)

Kite is known for his boogaloo and great footwork

- Has a very fun personality thus often invited as a battle guest to battle other dancers

Jaygee (South Korea)

Jaygee is part of World Fame-Us crew which consist of many great poppers

- Has a unique dance style which uses the scarecrow and boogaloo, making his dance very loose

- Also often invited as a DJ for many dance competitions in Korea
